Hi! I'm Max.

I'm a Russian-born, American-raised aspiring product manager based in Brooklyn, New York.

My passion in life is to create impactive, memorable experiences. I do this through programming projects and composing music. In the past, I've coded a tool to log and track fitness progress, co-founded and ran a 35-piece orchestra, started and managed a $1700 crowdfunding campaign inspired by a Humans of New York photo, collaborated on an online text-based multiplayer game, and freelanced as a film composer.

I'm a people person first and an analytical thinker second. I spend a lot of time thinking about product features and understanding and empathizing with the users before I start building. I am not an engineer; to me code and music theory are both tools to help communicate my vision and impact my audience.

I currently work on the Product Operations team at Codecademy, and previously managed hardware & technology outreach at Indiegogo.
