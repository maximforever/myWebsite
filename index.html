Maxim Pekarsky

Hi! I'm Max.

I'm a Russian-born, New York-raised aspiring product manager based in Brooklyn, NYC

My passion in life is to create impactive, memorable experiences. I do this by programming projects and composing music. In the past, I've created an online tool to log and track fitness progress, co-founded and ran a 35-piece composers' orchestra, started and managed a $1700 crowdfunding campaign inspired by a Humans of New York photo, collaborated on a web turn-based multiplayer game, and freelanced as a film composer.

I always think about people first and tools second. Before I start building, I spend a lot of time trying to develop empathy for my users. Then, I work with code and instruments to best communicate my vision and impact my audience.

I am currently helping build Codecademy Pro and Ready on the Product Operations team at Codecademy, and previously worked to help the most innovative tech projects raise money as a Manager of Hardware & Technology outreach at Indiegogo.
